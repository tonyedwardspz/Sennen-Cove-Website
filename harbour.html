Sennen Harbour

Sennen Cove Harbour supports a mix of commercial fishing boats and small pleasure-fishing boats. It offers all harbour facilities for Â£10 a day to visiting craft from the harbour master. The approach from seaward is hazardous and should not be attempted without local knowledge / assistance.

The harbours location offers a scenic costal walk to the beach and onwards along the coastal path. The area is popular with diving groups for day trips.


Facilities

  • Pay and Display Carpark
  • Discount 24hr parking rate for visitors
  • Public toilets
  • Electric winch to haul boats onto the slipway
  • Emergency Tractor

Harbour Commission

The Harbour is a Trust Port and was established by a Parliamentary Order in 1907. The Harbour is administered by a board of Commissioners. Members are drawn from Harbour users, local authorities and organisations. The sea area of jurisdiction of the Harbour extends from Aire Point to the Irish Lady Rock.
