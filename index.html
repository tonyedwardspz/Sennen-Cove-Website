Sennen Cove

Sennen is a coastal village located on the south western tip of Cornwall, England. Sennen is popular with locals and visitors for a variety of reasons;.

  • 1.4km sandy beach
  • Consistant Surf Conditions
  • The Lifeboat
  • Nearby diving locations

Winter storms hit Sennen

Sennen Cove has been battered by the stormy atlantic all winter. 35ft waves have been breaking into the cliffs. Thankfully there has been no local loss of life.
