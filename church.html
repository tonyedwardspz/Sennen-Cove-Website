Saint Sennen Church

There has been a religious building on the site since the sixth century, possibly 520 AD. The greater part of the present building dates from the thirteenth century.

Visitors are welcome at all times, but you will find a particularly warm welcome at our services on Sundays and Thursdays.

If you want to know more about the life of the Church, want help and advice or just a chat, contact any of the people listed opposite.
