Surfing

Sennen is home to a world renowned surfing beach. The cove has one of the most consistent beach breaks in the UK. Warm waters allow for long days out on the waves throughout summer

The beach offers a safe place to surf for both beginners and pros. There are a number of surf schools run by profressionals with nearby facilities.

  • Beach side cafe, bar and toilets
  • Lifeguards during the summer season
  • Local parking
